## Design Notes

**Color scheme suggestion:** Dark navy background (#1a1a2e) with white text and accent colors matching osmAPI/osmTalk branding.

**Typography:** Clean sans-serif (Inter, Poppins, or SF Pro). Headlines bold. Body regular.

**Imagery:** Minimal. Let the live demos do the visual work. Use icons over photos.

**Animations:** Keep simple. Slide transitions: none or subtle fade. Element animations: appear on click (for progressive reveals).

**Total slides:** ~35. Most are displayed briefly (transitions, breaks, rubrics). Core content is maybe 15 slides. The rest of the time, you're doing LIVE DEMOS, not showing slides.
